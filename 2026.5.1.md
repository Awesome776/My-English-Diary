# May 1, 2026--Friday. Sunny.
This time I went for the holiday in my brother-in-law's car again. Like last time was last year's National Day holiday, we set off in the evening and arrived home early the next morning. But unlike last time, this trip was quite smooth--no **traffic jams** or unexpected problems. I still remember last time was really terrible: there was a lot of traffic, and there was even an accident. We were driving just fine on the highway, but then someone suddenly **cut us off** and forced us off the highway. After that, we had to follow the GPS through national roads and country roads. And that night there was also **heavy fog**. On that small country road, there was only our car. At that moment, my brother-in-law and I both felt a little scared. But in the end, we made it home safely--it just took a little longer. Now thinking back, it's actually pretty funny, haha.

This time, we got home at 4 a.m. As soon as I entered my room, I fell right asleep. I slept until 12 p.m.--finally, the first day of my May Day holiday began!!!

At noon, my sister and brother-in-law came over with their daughter for lunch. I saw my **niece**, whom I hadn't seen for a while. Her **nickname** is "Duomi". In just a few months, she has already learned to walk. But it seemed like she didn't really recognize me anymore--that made me a little sad.

Around 3 or 4 p.m., my **aunt** came to visit. They (my sister and aunt) went out shopping together. The weather today was really nice--bright and sunny. I had other plans for the evening, and also because I didn't sleep well last night, I didn't go with them. Instead, like my brother-in-law, I continued to catch up on sleep.

At about 5:30 p.m., I rode my dad's **electric scooter** to meet a friend for dinner. The place we went to was a spicy hot pot (malatang) stall recommended by my friend. It was really good--affordable and tasty.

After dinner, we kept riding and went to play **volleyball**. But I haven't played for too long--I'm out of practice and didn't play very well. ╥﹏╥
